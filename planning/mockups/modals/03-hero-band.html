Hero Band.

A 6px colored band along the very top of the card carries the type. No illustrations. Inside, a small square icon tile + monospace eyebrow micro-label echoes the band color. Plus Jakarta Sans for the title gives a confident, structured feel; JetBrains Mono is reserved for micro-labels and form-field captions, which keeps the system reading as “design-system,” not Bootstrap.
