Editorial Minimal.

A refined, editorial direction. A 3-px colored left bar plus an uppercase micro-eyebrow communicate the type quietly. Fraunces serif for the title gives personality without shouting; Manrope handles the body. Generous breathing room. The accent appears in three places only — bar, eyebrow, primary button — so the color reads as information, not decoration.
