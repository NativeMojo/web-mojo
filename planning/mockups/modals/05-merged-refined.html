// LONG form — the test: tinted vs neutral

The key A/B. Same edit-profile form, side-by-side. Left: info-tinted bg (what Modal.alert/showForm with type:info would produce). Right: neutral chrome (what Modal.show / Modal.showForm without a type would produce). My read after building both: the tint at 5% is gentle enough that even a long form reads fine — but neutral feels more focused for forms with no semantic type. Recommendation: keep the tint+band reserved for typed alerts and confirms; use the neutral chrome for showForm/showModelView/Modal.show.
